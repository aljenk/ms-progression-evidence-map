# Medical Disclaimer

This repository contains research information for evidence mapping around multiple sclerosis progression.

It is not medical advice, diagnosis, prognosis, treatment guidance or a recommendation to start, stop or change medication. Treatment decisions should always be discussed with a qualified clinician.

Clinical trial results apply to the studied population, intervention, comparator, outcome measures and follow-up period. Biomarkers and imaging findings require clinical context. Computational and structural biology sources, including AlphaFold, AlphaFold 3 and AlphaMissense, are hypothesis and prioritization tools. They are not clinical proof of treatment effect.

Repurposing signals are discussed only as research questions. They are not do-it-yourself treatment advice.
